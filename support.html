FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Yes — TwoOfUs uses Sign in with Apple. It’s the only sign-in method, so there’s no separate password to remember. Your Apple ID keeps your couple’s data private and synced across your devices through iCloud.

On the Home Screen, touch and hold an empty area until the icons jiggle, tap the + (or Edit → Add Widget), search for TwoOfUs, choose a size, and place it. For the Lock Screen, long-press the Lock Screen, tap Customize, select a widget slot, and add the TwoOfUs widget you want.

One of you generates an invite code in the format XXXX-XXXX. Share it with your partner, who enters it in their app. Once it’s accepted, your two devices are paired and your widgets start syncing.

The Distance widget needs location permission to work out how far apart you are. Open Settings → TwoOfUs → Location and make sure access is enabled. Both partners need location turned on for the live distance to appear.

You share one note between the two of you, up to 100 characters. When you write a new note it replaces the previous one and pushes a notification to your partner — so a quick “thinking of you” lands right on their screen.

Ask your partner to confirm that notifications are allowed under Settings → TwoOfUs → Notifications, that a Focus or Do Not Disturb mode isn’t silencing them, and that they’re signed in and still paired with you. A stable internet connection on both sides helps the note sync promptly.

Open the app, choose the Countdown widget, and pick a shared date — an anniversary, a trip, a reunion. Add a label and it’ll show the days remaining. The countdown syncs to both of you and updates automatically each day.

The Partner clock shows your partner’s current local time along with their name and city — ideal for long-distance couples in different time zones. It’s derived from their location and updates every minute, so you always know whether it’s morning or midnight on their side.

“Always” lets the Distance and Partner clock widgets stay accurate even when the app is closed, so your widgets reflect where you really are. “While Using” still works, but only updates while the app is open, which can leave widget info a little stale. For the smoothest experience we recommend “Always.”

You can sign out at any time under Settings → Account inside the app. For permanent deletion of your data, email us at jakobsworld@web.de and we’ll take care of it.

TwoOfUs requires iOS 26 or later. As long as your iPhone is running iOS 26 or newer, you’re good to go.
