Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

In short: TwoOfUs is built for exactly two people. Your data lives in your own iCloud and is shared only with the partner you connect to — never with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third party. We never ask for an email or password (Sign in with Apple handles that), we do not track you across apps, and we do not sell data. You can sign out and delete everything from inside the app at any time.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data the TwoOfUs iOS app ("TwoOfUs", "the app", "we", "us") processes, why, on what legal basis, and what rights you have. It is written to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. What data we collect

TwoOfUs collects only the minimum data needed to run its shared widgets for you and your partner:

  • Apple User Identifier — a stable, app-specific ID provided by Sign in with Apple. It lets us recognize your account without ever handling an email address or password.
  • Profile name / initials — the display name you choose, shown to your partner and used to render initials in the app and widgets.
  • Location (optional) — your precise coordinates, used solely to compute the live distance for the Distance widget. If you grant "Always" permission, the app may use background significant-location updates so the distance stays current even when the app is closed. Location is only collected if you enable the feature and grant permission.
  • Shared note — one short shared note (up to 100 characters) that you and your partner can update. Updating it sends a push notification to your partner.
  • Countdown — the target date and optional label for your shared Countdown widget.
  • Streak counter — an in-app counter of consecutive active days. This is an in-app feature only, not a standalone widget.
  • Partner clock (Partner-Uhr) data — to show your partner's current local time in the Partner-Uhr widget, the app derives the partner's time zone from their city or coordinates and displays it alongside their name and city.
  • Pairing invite code — a short code in the format XXXX-XXXX, used once to securely connect you with your partner.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • No email address or password — authentication is handled entirely by Apple's Sign in with Apple.
  • No payment or billing information.
  • No advertising identifiers, and no ads.
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We never sell or rent your data to anyone.

4. How we use the data

We use the data listed above exclusively to provide the app's core functionality: to authenticate you, connect you with your chosen partner, and power the shared widgets — Distance, Countdown, Note, and the Partner-Uhr (your partner's local time). The shared note also triggers a push notification to your partner when you update it. We do not use your data for profiling, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to running these features.

5. Legal basis (GDPR)

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — performance of a contract: processing your account identifier, profile, pairing, note, countdown, streak, and partner-clock data is necessary to provide the service you requested.
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR — consent: location data for the Distance widget is processed only with your explicit consent, given through the iOS location permission prompt. You may withdraw this consent at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interests: ensuring the security and integrity of the service and preventing abuse.

6. Infrastructure & data sharing

TwoOfUs stores your data using Apple iCloud (CloudKit). Apple Inc. acts as our processor and hosts the data on your behalf. Sign in with Apple and push notifications are likewise provided by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to these services: apple.com/privacy.

Your couple data is shared only with the single partner you connect with through your pairing code. It is not shared with other users, advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties. We do not access your data for any purpose beyond operating and supporting the app.

7. Data retention

We keep your data only as long as your account exists and you use the app. Your shared note, countdown, and profile persist so both partners can see them. When you sign out and delete your account (see Section 9), the associated data is removed. Backups held by Apple's iCloud infrastructure follow Apple's own retention and deletion cycles.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you (Art. 15);
  • request rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16);
  • request erasure of your data (Art. 17);
  • request restriction of processing (Art. 18);
  • data portability (Art. 20);
  • object to processing based on legitimate interests (Art. 21);
  • withdraw any consent at any time, without affecting prior lawful processing (Art. 7(3));
  • lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jakobsworld@web.de.

9. Sign out & deletion

You are always in control. Open the app and go to Settings → Account to sign out or to delete your account and its data. Deleting your account removes your profile and unpairs you from your partner. You can also revoke the app's access to Sign in with Apple in your iOS Settings under your Apple Account.

10. Children's privacy

TwoOfUs is not directed at children. The app is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Location permissions

The Distance widget requires location access to calculate how far apart you and your partner are. iOS will ask you to grant permission; you can choose "While Using" or "Always". "Always" enables background significant-location updates so the distance stays fresh even when the app is closed. You can change or revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → TwoOfUs. Disabling location simply turns off the Distance feature; the rest of the app keeps working.

12. Push notifications

When your partner updates the shared note, TwoOfUs sends you a push notification so you see it right away. Notifications are delivered via Apple Push Notification service. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → TwoOfUs.

13. Security

Data is encrypted in transit between your device and Apple's iCloud servers. Access to your couple data is limited to you and your paired partner; no one else can read your note, distance, countdown, streak, or partner-clock information. We rely on Apple's infrastructure security together with the app's client-side access controls.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves or as legal requirements change. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Please review it periodically.
